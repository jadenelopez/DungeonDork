Description

Henry is a quiet, easy going, and dertermined rogue. Starting with self taught training to finding his master Croc (see Side charactes), Henry has become a silent and efficient member of the party that uses stealth to his advantage. He wears a hoodie and facemask with loose movable camo pants with boots and a toolbelt containing knife holsters and slots for other equipment. Half of his upper body is covered in an undying purple demon flame. He was given the nickname "smiley" because of a nervous tick he developed from the demon world, where he smiles when he gets nervous. Some of Henry's attacks include stealth attack to deal big damage to his opponents as well demon mode, a rage like state where he gains similar abilitys to the demons that destroyed his village (see Story page). Henry's ultimate goal is to defeat the demons that destroyed his village and kill off as many of them as he can.

Description

Lavender’s Proxy is a golem created for the sole purpose of committing “heroic” acts. Exactly what this means is up to him, and with his growth towards becoming his own person this directive is in flux. Proxy is a dirt golem made with magic to keep him together. He has vines and various plants that grow off him, with a key hole in his chest with a memorial plate dedicated to his creators daughter. During proxys journey, he met with 3 naturalists to expand his abilitys and further his magical knowledge.
